Revenue Growth Trend

What to check: Is revenue growing year-over-year?

Why important: Shows market demand and business expansion.

Investor Tip: Avoid companies with flat or declining revenue for 2+ years.

2

Raw Material Dependency

What it means: Are inputs imported or locally sourced?

Why important:

  • Imported = forex risk + price volatility.
  • Local = more stable costs.

Example: Cement industry relies heavily on imported clinker if not vertically integrated.
