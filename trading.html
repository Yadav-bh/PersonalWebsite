The Trading sector includes companies that engage in import, export, wholesale, and retail trade of goods. These are primarily distributor or merchant companies involved in selling goods, often imported, within Nepal.

‚úÖ Important Points to Analyze in Trading Companies

1

Revenue Source and Volume

Trading companies rely on sales volume and distribution channels.

  • Check annual revenue trends and sales diversification.
  • High dependency on a single product or country is risky.
2

Profit Margin

Margins are usually low compared to manufacturing or banking.

Look at:

  • Gross Margin
  • Net Profit Margin
  • Operating efficiency
3

Import Dependency & Currency Risk

As most goods are imported, these firms are vulnerable to:

  • Exchange rate fluctuations (USD/NPR)
  • Import restrictions or government policies
  • Logistic and fuel costs
