The hotel sector is cyclical, highly sensitive to tourism trends, political stability, and global economic factors. But it offers high growth potential and capital gain opportunities when tourism booms.

1

Occupancy Rate

What it means: Percentage of rooms occupied vs available.

Why important: Directly affects revenue.

Investor Tip: A growing occupancy trend signals strong performance.

2

Average Room Rate (ARR)

What it means: Average income earned per occupied room.

Why important: Higher ARR = better pricing power.

Look for: Year-on-year growth in ARR and seasonal spikes.

3
