The "Others" category in NEPSE includes companies that don't fall under traditional sectors like banking, insurance, hydropower, etc. These businesses usually operate in diverse fields such as holding companies, service providers, technology firms, logistics, credit rating agencies, market infrastructures, stock depository companies, and investment holding firms.
